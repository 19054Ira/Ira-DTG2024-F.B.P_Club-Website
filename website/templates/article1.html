2024 NGA SEASON BEGINS

Posted on May 3, 2024 by Ira Jequantavious Smith III Abesamis

A new season has now arrived for the NGA team after a very successful run last season. After a tough loss in the semi-finals the NGA hopes to finally get over the hump and make a run to the finals in this new season. Along with the new players that could potentially be drafted by the NGA this season is coming to be one of the best seasons for the NGA.

Key Dates:

May 8: 2024 NGA player drafts (10:30am NZST)

May 10: 1st NGA player training(5PM - 6:30PM NZST)

May 13: 1st season game (3PM - 4PM NZST)

May 15: NGA open training day (5PM - 6:30PM NZST)

May 20: 1st In-season tournament game(5PM - 6:30PM NZST)
